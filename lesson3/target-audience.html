Persona 1

A soccer mom who cares deeply for her three children. She wants them to be safe, happy, and healthy. Part of that involves checking the weather so they don't catch a cold, or whether or not soccer practice will be canceled.

Persona 2

A team lead on a contstruction crew, he must notify his crew if they will be working or not. His company focuses on building new houses, so working out in the rain or snow isn't the greatest option.

Scenarios

  1. The bad weather will affect my team's ability to work today. How bad is it really?
  2. I am hoping to take my kid's to soccer practice today so that my husband and I can go out to dinner.
  3. If there is a snow day today, the kids won't be able to go to school and I'll have to find a sitter if I can still go to work.
  4. Should I bring an umbrella to the job site today?
  5. If I go on a vacation next week, will my house freeze over? Do I need to keep the heater running?
