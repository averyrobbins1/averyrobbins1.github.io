Design Principles Document

Fitt's Law

M1 Finance

m1finance.com

M1 Finance's goal is to get the user to create an account and start investing on their platform. Therefore, following Fitt's law, M1 Finance put a relatively large "Get Started" button right in the middle of the page.

Contrast

Church of Jesus Christ

churchofjesuschrist.org

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints wants young adult users to remember about and participate in this devotional by Elder and Sister Gong. The image behind the announcement is darkened, and the text is white. This provides nice contrast and draws the user's attention.

Hick's Law

UVA Data Science

datascience.virginia.edu

UVA wants users who come to this section of their site to join their Data Science Masters program. They do not want a cluttered page that has a lot of options to choose from. They either want the user to learn more or apply to the program, so those are the buttons available.
